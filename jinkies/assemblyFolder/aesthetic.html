Sahil, Rebecca, Pulkit

The mystery manor adventure takes place in the Victorian era, so we designed the aesthetics of the window in this fashion. We took our inspiration from the windows from the Endicott House and some common victorian manor windows.

The first important feature is the stained Victorian glass. We drew and painted our stained Victorian glass using a fall colour palette glass paint to fit the time period as a decorative element. It also does a beautiful job as scattering the light and creating a mysterious atmosphere.

The second feature is the antique wooden frame out of molding and pine wood that were stained to a Dark Walnut colour afterwards. It was based from the windows in Endicott House living room, especially the radiator pattern at the window base.

The last feature is the curtains on the window. Most of the curtains are usually darker in Victorian houses. However from the user feedback, a white coloured curtain created a spookier effect because it lights up with the thunder & lightning effect.
