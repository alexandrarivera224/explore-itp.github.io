Logistics

  • Setting the Scene (with sound)

    "Wow, this is cool!" The guests enter the room. They hear lightning in the background. They begin to explore the environment. A Victorian setting with large paintings and tall bookcases sets the tone.


  • Lights Out!

    The chandelier begins to flicker. "Uh oh!" The lights go out...


  • Lightning Strikes and Thunder Rolls

    A dim light shines in the corner. Lightning illuminates the room through the large Victorian window.


  • Clues!

    Clues for a game can also be revealed when the lightning flashes. They are hidden on objects all around the room. A large clue on the portrait can trigger the hunt.
